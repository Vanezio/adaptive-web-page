Initiating Brand®2*†

Patients with T2DM

Patients with T1DM

Insulin-naïve

  • Calculate total daily insulin dose as 0.2–0.4 Units/kg/day
    • Initiate Brand® as 1/3–1/2 of total daily insulin requirement
    • Administer remainder as mealtime insulin
  • Start with 10 Units followed by individual dosage adjustments

Switching from another basal insulin

  • Reduce dose by 20%
  • 1:1 from once-daily basal insulin
  • Reduce dose by 20% from twice-daily basal insulin or Brand®
  • Close glucose monitoring is recommended during the transfer and in the following weeks
  • If needed, adjust doses and timing of concurrent mealtime insulin or antidiabetic treatments

Missed dose†

If your patients missed a dose of Brand®:

  • Instruct them to inject their daily dose as soon as they remember
  • Instruct them to ensure that at least 8 hours have passed between consecutive Brand® injections before returning to their usual dosing schedule

Convenient once-daily dosing2*§

Brand® should be dosed once daily, preferably at the same time each day. When administration at the same time of day is not possible, Brand® allows for flexibility in the timing of insulin administration, with a minimum of 8 hours between doses.

For more dosage and administration instructions, please refer to the Brand Monograph.

Indication:

Brand® is indicated for once-daily treatment of adults with diabetes mellitus to improve glycemic control.
Brand® is also indicated for the treatment of pediatric patients (>2 years old) with type 1 diabetes mellitus.

Clinical use:

Not recommended for treatment of diabetic ketoacidosis.

Contraindication:

  • During episodes of hypoglycemia

Most serious warnings and precautions:

Hypoglycemia is the most common adverse effect of insulin

  • Glucose monitoring is required
  • Uncorrected hypo- or hyperglycemic reactions can cause loss of consciousness, coma and even death
  • Use caution and medical supervision when converting insulin products

Administration

  • Inspect visually prior to administration and only use if solution appears clear and colourless
  • Do not mix with any other insulin, administer intravenously or use in insulin infusion pumps

Other relevant warnings and precautions:

  • Refer to respective product monographs for concomitant oral antidiabetic agents for their warnings and precautions
  • Stress or concomitant illness, especially infections and febrile conditions, may change insulin requirements
  • Brand® should not be diluted
  • Combination with thiazolidinediones (TZD) not indicated in type 2 diabetes; can cause heart failure and edema
  • Risks associated with sharing insulin delivery devices
  • Hyglycemia
  • Risk of hypokalemia
  • Dosing may need to be adjusted in patients with renal and/or hepatic impairment
  • Risk of immune responses (e.g., systemic allergic reactions or antibody production)
  • Pregnant and nursing women
  • Pediatrics
  • Geriatrics
